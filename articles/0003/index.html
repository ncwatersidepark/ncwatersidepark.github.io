Waterside Park signs Treaty of Mutual Recognition with Vishwamitra.

From today, Waterside Park will enter into bilateral recognition with the State of Vishwamitra.

In a treaty signed today, which can be seen below, the State of Vishwamitra and Waterside Park recognised each others' sovereignty.
